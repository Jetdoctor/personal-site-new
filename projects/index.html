Business Intelligence · CFN Kimya · 2024

Replacing manual reporting with a company-wide BI landscape

The problem: reporting across the company was manual, fragmented, and slow. Teams in five departments were assembling numbers by hand every week, and leadership lacked a shared, current view of performance.

What I did: gathered requirements through stakeholder interviews and process mapping, defined the KPI structure with each department, and designed and delivered a suite of Power BI dashboards in collaboration with IT, then iterated on them with the actual business users.

Change Management · Olympus R&D · 2024–2025

Making continuous improvement visible in a global R&D organization

The problem: Lean, Agile, and Six Sigma initiatives across a global R&D organization lacked a unified way to be tracked, compared, and governed. Improvement work was happening, but its status and impact were hard to see.

What I did: supported the development of a global Control Tower framework, including KPI structure definition and governance alignment; documented and integrated Lean, Agile, and DFSS methodologies within the Center of Development Excellence; applied GenAI tools to speed up improvement-idea evaluation and pre-categorization; and built capability management and initiative tracking tools.
